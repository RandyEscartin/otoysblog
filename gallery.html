The 10,000 Roses Cafe and More is a popular attraction in Cordova, Cebu, Philippines. Mangrove Forest

Cordova in Cebu, Philippines, is known for its mangrove forests that play a crucial role in supporting biodiversity and maintaining ecological balance. These mangrove ecosystems provide habitats for various marine life, serve as nurseries for fish, and contribute to shoreline protection.
